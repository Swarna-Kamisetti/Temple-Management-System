Ganesh Chaturthi


Statue of Ganesha at temple

Ganesh Chaturthi, also called Vinayaka Chavithi, is an auspicious Hindu festival which is celebrated every year.
The festival is celebrated in the Bhadra month as per the Hindu calendar which generally falls in mid-August to September.
It marks the birthday of Lord Ganesha.

Every year Ganesh Chaturthi is celebrated at our temple. Poojas are performed to the Idol of Ganesha that is installed in Temple.
These poojas are conducted for eleven days and nimajjan is done on 12th day.
